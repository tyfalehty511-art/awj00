# **App Name**: Awj

## Core Features:

- Merchant Authentication & Store Setup: Secure merchant authentication and registration, allowing each merchant to establish their unique online store and obtain a dedicated subdomain.
- Merchant Dashboard: A streamlined and intuitive dashboard for merchants to oversee store analytics, manage orders, and control overall store settings.
- Product Management Interface: Empower merchants to easily add, edit, and delete products, including specifying product name, price, description, and categorizing them as digital or physical.
- AI Product Description Assistant: An integrated AI tool to automatically generate engaging and detailed product descriptions based on core product information, accelerating the listing process.
- Dynamic Multi-Tenant Storefronts: Automatically renders unique public-facing e-commerce storefronts for each merchant, accessible via their dedicated subdomain.
- Subdomain Multi-Tenancy Routing: Backend logic to correctly route incoming requests to the appropriate merchant's store based on the subdomain of the URL.
- Supabase Integration for Data & Auth: Leverages Supabase for robust and scalable database management for all product, store, and user data, alongside secure user authentication services.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Palette: Dark scheme to convey sophistication and modernity. Primary brand color: a vibrant yet refined indigo (#6168F0) representing ambition and digital innovation. Background: a subtle, deep blue-grey (#202126) for a sleek backdrop. Accent color: a crisp sky blue (#6BCEF5) to highlight interactive elements and draw attention.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-inspired, and impactful appearance. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, highly readable, and neutral text that scales well across different content types, fitting a functional SaaS platform.
- Minimalist and clean line icons with a slightly rounded aesthetic to maintain a contemporary, user-friendly feel. Icons should clearly convey their function without clutter.
- The layout should be highly responsive, adaptable for both desktop and mobile, emphasizing clean lines, generous white space, and clear hierarchy. Dashboards will feature a sidebar navigation and content organized into intuitive cards.
- Subtle, fast-paced transitions and micro-interactions on hover and click actions to enhance user engagement and provide immediate feedback without distracting from content. Focus on smoothness and efficiency.
